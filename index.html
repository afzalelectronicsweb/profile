INTRODUCTION

Founded in 1992, Afzal Electronics is a sole proprietorship business with a humble beginning that developed into national brand in retail of consumer appliances, mobile and motor bikes. Under the leadership of Mr. Waseem Afzal (CEO Afzal Electronics) the group has witnessed exponential growth in the last decade. The chain expended from single branch, back in 2007, to nationwide chain of 186 branches of consumer appliances, mobiles and motor bikes. 
The Group head office is situated at TAMC building, Gulberg III, MM Alam Road, Lahore.

 

ABOUT US

We believe that sustainable success requires uncompromising commitment coupled with customer centric approach and relentless pursuit of perfection. Our decades-long experience allows us to embrace and foster new, forward thinking business practices. Our customer-first philosophy is seamlessly blended in our corporate DNA. We only deal in top market brands with the objective to provide quality products and after sale services that in turn is helping us to develop our customer base.
